About

“Security is a Choice”


Conceptualised years before its creation, Secure Hours is a blog website which aims to tackle insecurity in both cyber space and psychology. All the content is shared with the view of providing a
new perspective on various topics and inviting healthy discussions. The vision of Secure Hours is to create a collaborative community of self-motivated, aware and ethical individuals.

In this blog, readers will find a conglomeration of discussions on technology and information security, along with art and photography sections to satisfy the writer's desperate need for recreation.
