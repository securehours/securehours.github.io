Cybersecurity Awareness Month

October 8, 2021

This is one of those topics that possesses the rare quality of being extremely popular yet equally neglected by the masses. Cybersecurity is a subject so versatile that, even eighteen years after a month was inducted after it, the world is unaware of its vitality. Increasing digital speed and the lack of patience is degrading the general wariness of most individuals – while the remaining individuals are becoming more innovative and cunning when it comes to finding new ways to breach security walls for the thrill of it.

The little grey area in between is where security geeks thrive.

This is an account of how Secure Hours celebrated Cybersecurity Awareness Month over the years so far.




2022

The October of 2022 was spent in making ourselves more aware of cybersecurity. Secure Hours' second 'CAM' was spent in the company of some of the most wonderful cybersecurity enthusiasts, as we upskilled and solved challenges together.


2021

After spending the first six days of October struggling to find an idea to celebrate what would officially be Secure Hours’ first ‘CAM’, an idea struck on the seventh afternoon. Every Thursday, all month long, a fresh ‘Pro Tip’ was shared on social media to inform peers about useful security measures and precautions to prevent common security breaches while surfing the internet. A small step, but it was a start.
