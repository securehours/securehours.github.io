Herjavec Group and Fishtech Group join forces

January 1, 2022

In the dusk of 2021, an exciting company merger was announced. Herjavec Group, the cybersecurity services company headed by IT Entrepreneur Robert Herjavec, is joining forces with Fishtech Group, cybersecurity solutions provider led by CEO Gary Fish, to form a security solutions powerhouse. Backed by Apax Partners LLP, who will be the major stakeholders in the new business, this deal will see Shark Tank star Herjavec serving as CEO of the company while cybersecurity visionary Fish will take up the position of Chairman of the Board.

Both Herjavec Group and Fishtech Group are major award-winning cybersecurity solution providers, and having the two innovative businesses operating as a single entity will undoubtedly be a game-changer. The agreement brings together the “complementary strengths of both organisations, resulting in an industry powerhouse with a broad, holistic suite of best-in-class managed detection and response capabilities, professional services, and identity offerings with a global perspective to address enterprise customers’ increasingly complex information security needs.”

Consumers will certainly benefit a great deal from this merger. Herjavec Group pioneers in cloud and tech-enabled co-managed Security Information and Event Management (SIEM). Fishtech Group is a market leader in enterprise Managed Detection and Response (MDR). Joining forces brings the best of both under a single umbrella. The new brand, which is to be announced later in 2022, promises unparalleled security and cloud expertise with a focus on driving security maturity as its competitive differentiator.

Naturally, the union of two mega-brands will bring a huge consumer base under a unified system and policy. With six major security operations centers (SOCs) all over the globe, the combined company is set to have one of the largest managed security engineering teams entirely dedicated to delivering innovative solutions to enterprise clients. The two giants complement each other in their individually offered services, and with the decades of friendship between Herjavec and Fish, the new company could potentially become the undisputed leader in cybersecurity services in the world, thus transforming the landscape and redefining the market as a whole.
